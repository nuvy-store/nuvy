Questions

Good to know

Do you ship across Morocco?

Yes — we ship to every city in Morocco. Delivery usually takes 2–4 business days depending on your location.

Are NUVY bites vegan?

Yes — every flavor is 100% plant-based, naturally sweetened with dates or maple syrup, and a good source of plant protein.

Do they contain allergens?

Most flavors contain tree nuts. Each product page lists its full ingredients — check before ordering if you have allergies.

How long do they stay fresh?

Up to 2 weeks at room temperature, or up to a month refrigerated.

How do I pay?

Every flavor has an "Order on WhatsApp" button. Message us there and we'll confirm your total and send our bank transfer details — no card or account needed.
